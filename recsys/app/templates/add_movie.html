  • What happens to the movie added?

    • Semantic analysis will be performed on the description.
    • Text analsys will be performed on the description.
    • Root word analysis will be performed on the description.
    • Movie will be compared to bookmarked movies.
    • Movie will be given a similarity index to each bookmarked movie.
    • The highest similarity index will be the final similarity index.
    • Movies appear in the horizontal scrolling pane in descending order of similarity index.
